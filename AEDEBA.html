What is AEDEBA All About?

AEDEBA (Association d'aide au development region rurale) is a non-government organization dedicated to creating better living conditions in rural areas and providing help where it is needed the most. AEDEBA is also active in facilitating and implementing direct investments, promoting and increasing exports, improving the competitiveness of African economy, as well as reputation, and economic and regional development of the African countries.

Our mission is to provide state of the art international expertise for public, private, non-profit and academic sectors. We achieve this mission through international transfer of know-how, networking of key stakeholders and through development and implementation of projects on regional, national and transnational level.

Our network includes a wide variety of institutions, NGOs and companies ready to join international UN-funded projects. We provide the network with partner search support, project development and project administration services.

AEDEBA forges throughout the Africa to support quality learning for every girl and boy, especially those in greatest danger of being left behind. We work with partners around the world to promote policies and expand access to services that protect all children. AEDEBA has helped reduce child mortality all over Africa by working to reach the most vulnerable children, everywhere.

AEDEBA Sections in Cameroon

Bafoussam
AEDEBA's Headquarters

AEDEBA was founded in 2002 in Bafoussam, Cameroon and its team brought over $40 million from UN funds to it's clients. AEDEBA has project partners in all African countries as well as many other states throughout the world. With such a network, it became a reliable partner for both developers and participants in international co-operation projects.

Throughout the Country
Field Offices

Through its field offices, AEDEBA develops strategies, programmes and activities in consultation with national authorities and other partners. AEDEBA also operates a number of specialized institutes and centres. The AEDEBA House hosts a wide variety of free events all year: concerts, shows, ceremonies, screenings, exhibitions and conferences.These events are open to the public.
